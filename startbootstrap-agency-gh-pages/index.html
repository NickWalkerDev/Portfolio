Featured Listing

1920's Tudor estate with the finest architectural pedigree reimagined without compromise: The Palmateer House by L. Milton Wolf.

A gated circular drive leads to a 5bd/6ba residence that flows through Tudor archways with oak floors, custom millwork and period correct detailing. A two-story foyer is flanked by formal dining and living room with bar and study. Great room features a chef's kitchen, dining alcove and family room. Butler's pantry, office/guest room and theater complete the first floor. A turret staircase and vestibule connect the bedroom suites, laundry and rec room loft. The master boasts vaulted ceilings, marble-clad bath, dual walk-in closets and private terrace.

Outside features a large dining patio above a lush yard, bluestone pool and spa, fire pit and entertainers pavilion with bar/grill. A convenient pool bath, private guest cottage, four-car garage and expansive basement round out a perfect blend of English countryside and Los Feliz living.
